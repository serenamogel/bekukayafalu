8 Under-$100 Winter Accessories an Editor Is Buying

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Today was the first time in a long time that I’ve seen snow, and I’ll admit that it was quite a treat walking my dog in a winter wonderland this morning, but with snow can come a drop in temperature. So, as a shopping editor, I’ve been using the weather as an excuse to shop for some new winter accessories. Furry bucket hats, luxe cashmere scarves, and faux leather gloves can take any basic puffer vest or wool coat to the next level while keeping you warm. I rounded up eight winter accessories from the Kate Spade Outlet, Lululemon, Ugg, J.Crew, and more — and prices start at just $25.

8 Winter Fashion Accessory Editor Picks:

J.Crew Cashmere Wrap Scarf

Getting hit with frosty weather makes it that much harder to get out of bed, but if I had this Cashmere Wrap from J. Crew, I might be more inclined to brave the outdoors. It comes in four chic colors: red, tan, gray, and black, and when draped over the shoulders, it falls below the knee. The scarf’s made from 100 percent cashmere, making it extra soft for bundling and super warm for fighting back against the winter chill.

Lululemon Fleece Everywhere Belt Bag

One of my favorite ways to add warmth to an outfit is with plush-textured fabric, and Lululemon heard me loud and clear when it designed this Fleece Everywhere Belt Bag. The crossbody belt comes in a white teddy material and features gold-toned hardware, including a buckle closure that can be adjusted to style across your chest or waist and two zipper compartments to easily store your essentials. This is a great winter bag, whether you’re active outdoors or just on the go.

Nordstrom Faux-Fur Bucket Hat

All summer long, I wore bucket hats because the brimmed style flattered my head shape, but once the weather cooled down, I had to swap my lighter hats for warmer beanie styles. That was until my favorite brands started swapping out summery materials for warmer, fuzzy styles. Take this Faux Fur Bucket Hat from Nordstrom for example. The short-brimmed hat comes in a lush pink and is lined with plush faux fur that’s sure to keep you warm while adding a fresh take on cold-weather fashion.
