Biography

Bebe Moore Campbell was born in Pennsylvania on February 18, 1950. The mother of In the House actress, Maia Campbell, she is also the New York Times bestselling author of What You Owe Me, Brothers and Sisters, and Singing in the Comeback Choir. Her novel, Your Blues Ain’t Like Mine, received the NAACP Image Award for Literature. Her daughter, Maia Campbell, starred for five seasons on the UPN sitcom, In the House. She studied elementary education at the University of Pittsburgh and subsequently moved to Los Angeles.
