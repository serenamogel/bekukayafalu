K.J. Osborn Wiki 

K. J. Osborn born on 10 June 1997 in Ypsilanti, Michigan is an American football wide receiver for the Minnesota Vikings of the NFL.

Osborn is a native of Ypsilanti, Michigan, and attended Lincoln High School before transferring to IMG Academy for his senior year. Osborn played at Buffalo for three years. He had 96 catches for 1,490 yards and 12 touchdowns.

K.J. Osborn College 

From Buffalo, K.J. Osborn transferred to Miami as a graduate transfer to the University of Miami in 2019.
