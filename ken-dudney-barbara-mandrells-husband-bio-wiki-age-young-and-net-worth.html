Career

Ken is a former musician and Navy pilot. At the time of their marriage, Dudney gave up his career as a Navy pilot to help further his wife’s career. He is a producer, known for Barbara Mandrell: The Lady Is a Champ (1983), Barbara Mandrell: Something Special (1985), and Barbara Mandrell’s Christmas: A Family Reunion (1986).

House

In 1988, Ken and his family built a log cabin mansion called the Fontanel Mansion. The home consisted of six bedrooms, 13 bathrooms, two kitchens, five fireplaces, and a helicopter landing pad. It has been considered to be the largest log cabin home in the world.

They sold the home in 2002 in order to downsize. The home was auctioned. It has since been turned into a tourist attraction with a restaurant, a hotel, an outdoor music venue, and an indoor shooting range.

Their daughter Jamie was the human resources manager of the mansion until February 2017. She then began a new career with a company that works to place people with addiction issues into appropriate rehabilitation facilities.
