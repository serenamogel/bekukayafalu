Emma Ellingsen YouTube

Ellingsen created her YouTube channel on November 5, 2014, to make beauty-related videos. She, regardless, made her first video on March 10, 2016, responding to fan questions collected from her Snapchat following. Her channel grew invariably as she continued to upload storytime videos, makeup videos, unboxing videos, Q&As, challenges, and vlogs in Norwegian in the following months.

Nevertheless, one of her makeup videos from early 2017 drew the concentration of many English-speaking people who asked her to make videos in English. While she already had about 40K subscribers, it was her first video in English, titled I’M TRANSGENDER, posted on June 25, 2017, in which she opened up about her adventure of being a transgender person, that gained her widespread popularity even outside Norway.

In the summer next year, she also signed with the Norwegian talent agency Heartbreak Management and took a year off from school to concentrate on modeling.
