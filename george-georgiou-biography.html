George Georgiou- Professional Life, Careers

George Georgiou kickstarted his career as a theater artist by working on the London stage in plays at the National Theatre, Theatre Royal S.E, and Haymarket Theatre.

Next in 2013, he made his on-screen debut by appearing in the role of Turkish Captain in the TV movie called, Byron.

Following that, he portrayed the character of Omar Nafoor in the series, Law & Order: UK. The other star casts of the series include Bradley Walsh, Jamie Bamber, Harriet Walter, Ben Daniels, Freema Agyeman, Bill Paterson, Dominic Rowan, and Peter Davison.

From 2013-2016, he depicted the character of Razdal mo Eraz in the American fantasy drama television series, Game of Thrones. This series was created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss and is based on the novel A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin.

Adding up more, Georgiou depicted the role of Yousef in the British teen sex comedy-drama streaming television series, Sex Education.

It became his breakthrough role as it earned him massive fame and recognition. The series plots the lives of students and adults in the fictional town of Moordale as they deal with various issues related to sexuality, identity, and relationships.

Talking about his recent project, in 2023, George starred as Kastor in episodes of the American fantasy television series, Carnival Row. The first season of this series aired on August 30, 2019, while the second season of this series ran from February 17, 2023, to March 17, 2023, along with 10 episodes.
