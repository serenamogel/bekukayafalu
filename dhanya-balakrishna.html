Some Lesser Known Facts About Dhanya Balakrishna

  • Dhanya Balakrishna is an Indian actress who has worked in various Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam films and TV series.
  • Before making her debut in the film industry, Dhanya used to be part of the theatre group Evam in Chennai.
  • Her father won the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award in 2021.
  • In 2012, she played the role of Rashmi in the film Kadhalil Sodhappuvadhu Yeppadi which was shot in both Tamil and Telugu language. The name of the Telugu adaptation was Love Failure.
  • In 2014, she appeared as Sanjeevani, the main lead, in the Telugu film Amrutham Chandamamalo.

    A screengrab from the film Amrutham Chandamamalo, featuring Dhanya Balakrishna

  • She is well-known for working in various films like the Tamil film Raja Rani (2013), Telugu film Raju Gari Gadhi (2015), and Jaya Janaki Nayaka (2017).
  • She played the role of Meera in the Tamil web series As I’m Suffering From Kadhal (2017) on Disney+ Hotstar.

    A screengrab from the TV series As I’m Suffering From Kadhal, starring Dhanya Balakrishna

  • She has worked in many web series including the Tamil comedy show What’s Up Velakkari (2019), the Telugu series Alludu Garu (2021), and the Telugu drama series Loser (2022).
  • In 2022, she appeared as the main lead, Swapna in the Tamil film Carbon.

    A screengrab from the film Carbon, starring Dhanya Balakrishna

  • In the same year, she was the associate director for an episode of the Amazon Prime Video TV series Putham Pudhu Kaalai: Vidiyaadha.
  • In 2023, she appeared in the Kannada film Undenama.

    A screengrab from the film Undenama, starring Dhanya Balakrishna

  • She runs a self-titled YouTube channel on which she posts videos about her routine and events happening in her life. She has more than seventy-five thousand subscribers on her YouTube channel.
  • She loves to travel and explore and often posts pictures from her travels on Instagram.

    A picture posted by Dhanya Balakrishna on social media from her trip to Scotland

  • She collaborates with beauty brands and often posts review videos on her YouTube channel.
