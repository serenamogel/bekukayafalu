8 Things You Didnt Know About Tej Sapru

October 2024 · 3 minute read

➡ Tej Sapru Biography

Tej Sapru, an Indian actor, works in Bollywood films and became popular for his villainous style and tremendous performances. He did many Hindi films and was active during the 80s to 90s. Among many hit films, some of the most popular films may include ‘Gupt’, Sirf Tum’, ‘Dil Tera Aashiq’, ‘Saajan’, and many more that are worth sharing to watch and the best is, he got much appreciation from his fellows, fans, and the viewers. Apart from movies, he has also worked in many television serials including ‘Qubool Hai’, ‘Saat Phere’, ‘Yahan Main Ghar Ghar Kheli’, and many more. However, there are so many things to know about him. So, let us have a look at these unknown things!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Tej Sapru

1- Comes from a family of Actors

An Indian actor, Tej Sapru who is known for his brilliant acting prowess comes from a family of actors as he was born to D. K. Sapru and Hemvati, both were actors in the Hindi film industry and became famous for their work.

2- Worked with Biggies

Tej Sapru got the chance to work with Bollywood biggies including Salman Khan, Madhuri Dixit Nene, Sanjay Dutt, Anupam Kher, Bobby Deol, Manisha Koirala, Kajol Devgan, and many others who are being famous for their outstanding performances.

3- He & Siblings

Tej Sapru has two sisters, named Priti Sapru who is working as a screenwriter and actress, and another one, Reema Rakesh Nath (writer and director) both are popular names of the Indian and Punjabi film industry. It seems Tej has a strong association with the Punjabi film industry as well.

4- Love for Sports

Tej Sapru has a deep love for sports as he loves watching and playing badminton and cricket during his free time and holidays as well. These activities make him productive throughout the day.

5- Child Artist

In 1965, Tej Sapru made his first appearance as a child artist in the Bollywood film titled Shaheed’. It seems he had a love and passion for acting since his early age and following his passion, he made his first on-screen appearance at a young age.

6- Multi-lingual

It is incredible to know that the actor has command over 12 languages including Hindi, English, Malayalam, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, Punjabi, Bengali, Marathi, Bhojpuri, and Rajasthani. And the best is, he has worked in multilingual films.

7- His Wife & Rekha

You will be amazed to know that Tej Sapru married Dhanalakshmi Sapru who is the younger sister of the Bollywood veteran actress, Rekha. And they share a strong bonding with each other.

8- Most Appreciated Performance

In 1979, the actor, Sapru gave a fantastic performance in the film titled ‘Surraksha’ in which he portrayed the role of an agent Jackson for which he got much appreciation and his performance was considered as most appreciated one.
