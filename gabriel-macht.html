He was born in an upper-middle-class family in The Bronx, New York City, New York, United States of America. He is an American and is of Russian, German, and Ukrainian descent.

Gabriel Macht's education

Gabriel graduated from Beverly Hills High School and joined Carnegie Mellon College of Fine Arts, from where he graduated in 1994. At Carnegie Mellon, he was a member of Delta Upsilon fraternity, a collegiate men's fraternity.

Gabriel Macht's personal life

On the 29th of December 2004, Gabriel got married to the love of his life, Jacinda Barrett. Jacinda Barrett is an actress and model. They had a romantic love affair for a few years before getting married in 2004.

As a married couple, they have been parents twice, first child, a girl, Satine Anais Geraldine Macht, and second child, a son, Luca.
